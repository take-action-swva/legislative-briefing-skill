## Virginia Senators

Load these early — senator press releases are often the fastest source
for Virginia-specific statistics. Check before searching elsewhere.

| Source | URL | Best For | Reliability | Last Verified |
|--------|-----|----------|-------------|---------------|
| Sen. Mark Warner | warner.senate.gov | Warner press releases, stated positions, VA-specific stats | primary | 2026-06-01 |
| Sen. Tim Kaine | kaine.senate.gov | Kaine press releases, stated positions, VA-specific stats | primary | 2026-06-01 |

**Session notes:**
- Warner and Kaine press releases on the SAVE Act contained Virginia-specific
  passport and name-change statistics that were not available elsewhere. Check
  official senator pages early for any bill affecting Virginia voters.
